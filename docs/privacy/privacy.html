Ensuring your account cannot be accessed

Only the designated kiosks have the required access tokens to interact with the API.

A user must be logged in to access or amend their balance - no purchases will be possible without logging in. While a single tap allows immediate log out, a user will be logged out of the service if they are inactive for 5 minutes.
